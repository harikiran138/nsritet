================================================================================
DESIGN DECISIONS
================================================================================

COLOR PALETTE:
- Corporate Navy (#0047BA): Primary branding color, high contrast
- Royal Blue (#007ACC): Secondary interactions, visual interest
- Light Blue (#2196F3): Accents and highlights
- Magenta (#FF007A): Tagline highlights, call attention
- Clean neutrals: White, grays for readability

TYPOGRAPHY:
- Font-black for hero impact (5xl-8xl)
- Font-bold for section headers (3xl-4xl)
- Font-light for body text (readable, elegant)
- Consistent sizing across components

SPACING:
- Generous vertical padding (py-24 desktop)
- Consistent horizontal padding (px-8)
- Gap between elements (gap-8 default)
- Better visual breathing room

ANIMATIONS:
- Smooth entrances (0.6-0.8s ease-out)
- Staggered children (0.15s delay)
- Continuous loops (2-10s ease-in-out)
- Interactive feedback (200-300ms)
